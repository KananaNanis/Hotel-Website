Welcome to Akofena International Hotel Ghana’s Premiere Luxury Hotel

Ideally situated in one of the most exclusive and prestigious areas in Accra, Akofena International is a new hotel set to deliver all the creature comforts you’d expect from a world-class luxury hotel, as well as unexpected extra touches to make your experience with us truly beyond compare. By combining modern luxury with the richness of traditional Ghanaian culture, we’ve created an exclusive retreat that features 16 individually custom-decorated luxury suites, each designed to feel more like a home and less like a hotel.
